Research

Research laboratories

The department hosts five specialised research labs. Students can join a lab in their fourth year of the engineering program for end-of-studies projects.

AI &; Machine Learning Lab

Research in deep learning, computer vision, NLP and reinforcement learning. Around 12 permanent researchers and 25 PhD candidates.

Head: Prof1

Cybersecurity Lab

Cryptography, hardware security, formal verification and security of distributed systems. Strong industrial partnerships with major French companies.

Head: Prof2

Networks &; Systems Lab

Software-defined networks, 5G, network virtualisation, traffic engineering and cloud-native architectures.

Head: Prof3

Data Science &; Information Systems

Big data, data quality, business intelligence and the ethics of data-driven decision making.

Head: Prof4
