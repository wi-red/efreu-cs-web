The department

A complete training in digital engineering

From the post-baccalaureate Bachelor program to the five-year engineering degree, the Computer Science Department prepares its students for the major challenges of the digital world: software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and networks.

01

A practical curriculum

Lab sessions, group projects, and case studies make up a large part of the courses. Students write real code from the first semester onwards.

02

Strong industry links

Internships, apprenticeship contracts, and partnerships with French and international companies in the IT and consulting sectors.

03

Research-oriented options

Five specialised research labs in AI, cybersecurity, networks, data science and embedded systems, open to students from the fourth year.

04

International exposure

Exchange programs with more than 150 partner universities in Europe, North America and Asia. Several courses are taught entirely in English.

05

Modern campus

Located in Villejuif, the campus offers computer labs, a fab lab, library and study rooms accessible from 8am to 10pm.

06

Active student life

Robotics, CTF cybersecurity team, entrepreneurship club, hackathons and weekly tech meetups organised by students.
